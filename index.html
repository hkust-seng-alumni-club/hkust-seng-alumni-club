Dear SENG Fresh Graduates,

Congratulations on becoming a Graduate in the 23rd Congregation Ceremony! As a member of the SENG community, we have the mission not only to provide you with a great experience when you were a student, but also hope to accompany you throughout the years ahead. On your graduation, in order to make this memorable moment more meaningful yet fun-filled, we have prepared a surprise for you. You will be offered 2 FREE Graduation-themed Photos at our designated photo-taking booths with your loved ones – family and friends.

The photo booths are located in the Engineering Commons. We wish these photos would represent a millstone captivating you and your dearest buddies’ hard work over the past few years and recall your precious memories when looking at it in future.

To extend our warmest welcome and once again heartfelt congratulations on your graduation, we would like to offer you a platform to stay in touch – SENG Alumni Club. Interested graduates may provide your personal information to enjoy expanding your network for social and career growth and stay connected with the SENG mates in the years to come. Just fill in the form below to join our alumni club and redeem the 2 FREE Graduation-themed photos! Remember to do this before 19 Nov!

Should you have any queries, please feel free to contact Miss Karen Yau of the DENG Office(2358-8446, egkarenyau@ust.hk)

See you at the 23rd Congregation on 20 Nov 2015 and have fun on the day!

Warmest Regards,
Communications and External Affairs Team
