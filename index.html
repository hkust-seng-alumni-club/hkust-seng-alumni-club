Dear SENG Fresh Graduates,

Congratulations on becoming a Graduate in the 23rd Congregation Ceremony! As a member of the SENG community, we have the mission not only to provide you with a great experience when you were a student, but also accompany you throughout the years ahead. On your graduation, we hope to add value by capturing your memorable moments yet providing you a fun-filled experience. You will be offered 2 FREE Graduation-themed Photos at our designated photo-taking booths with your loved ones – family and friends.

The photo booths are located at the Engineering Commons (2/F, Academic Concourse, lifts 27-28). We wish these photos would represent a milestone captivating you and your dearest buddies’ hard work over the past few years and recall your precious memories when looking at it in the future.

Established in 2014, the SENG Alumni Club is a social club that offers you different networking and enriching opportunities. We hope to take the Congregation day as a major recruitment initiative and extend our warmest welcome to you on this special day. We also hope your participation of SENG Alumni Club activities would enlarge your social circle for your further career or personal growth.

We are also excited to share with you that HKUST Engineering is placed No.16 worldwide by the prestigious Times Higher Education (THE) World University Rankings of Top 100 Universities for Engineering and Technology 2015-16 just last week. This is the best ever position achieved by any local university since the establishment of this league table in 2010 which makes us No.1 in the Greater China for five straight years. This is a result of concerted efforts. As a graduate of this renowned university, we are sure you would stay in touch with your Alma Mater and keep yourself abreast of more great news!

Just fill in the form below to join the SENG Alumni Club and redeem the 2 FREE Graduation-themed photos. You are recommended to register before this Thursday, 19 Nov, to shorten the waiting time for photo-taking. Otherwise, you may drop by to make an onsite registration on 20 Nov 2015 by 5:30pm.

Should you have any queries, please feel free to contact Miss Natalie Lau of the DENG Office (3469 2272, egnatalie@ust.hk). See you on this Friday!

Warmest Regards,
Communications and External Affairs Team
