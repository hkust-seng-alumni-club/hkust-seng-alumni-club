Dear 2016 SENG Graduates,

Congratulations on becoming a Graduate in the 24th Congregation Ceremony! On your graduation, as a member of the SENG community, we hope to add value by capturing your memorable moments with your loved ones – family and friends by offering you 2 FREE Graduation Photos at our designated photo-taking booth.

The photo booth is located at the Engineering Commons (2/F, Academic Concourse, lifts 27-28). We wish these photos would represent a milestone captivating you and your dearest buddies’ hard work over the past few years and recall your precious memories when looking at it in the future.

Established in 2014, the SENG Alumni Club is a social club that offers you different networking and enriching opportunities. We hope to take the Congregation day as a major recruitment initiative and extend our warmest welcome to you on this special day. We also wish your participation of SENG Alumni Club activities would enlarge your social circle for your further career or personal growth.

As a graduate of this renowned university, we are sure you would stay in touch with your Alma Mater and keep yourself abreast of our news!

Just fill in the form below to join the SENG Alumni Club and redeem the 2 FREE Graduation photos. You are recommended to register before 16 Nov (Wed), to shorten the waiting time for photo-taking. Otherwise, you may drop by to make an onsite registration on 17 Nov (9:45am-1:45pm) & 18 Nov (1pm - 5pm).

Should you have any queries, please feel free to contact Karen Yau of the DENG Office (2358 8446) (egkarenyau@ust.hk). See you on 17 Nov & 18 Nov!

Warmest Regards,
Communications and External Affairs Team
