Dearest Fresh Graduate Engineers,

Congratulations for being one of the graduates in the 22nd Congregation Ceremony! As a member of the SENG community, we have the mission not only to provide you with a great experience when you were a student, but we also hope to accompany you throughout the years ahead. We have prepared a surprise to make this congregation more meaningful, yet fun-filled. You will be offered 2 free Graduation-themed photos from our designated photo-taking booths, with your loved ones - family and friends. Also, we would like to extend our warm invitation to you to be a member of the SENG Alumni Club.

The photo booths are located in the Engineering Commons. We wish these photos would represent a milestone captivating you and your dearest buddies’ hard works over the past few years and recall good old memories when looking at it in future.

Of course, these photos don't mean the end of friendships and fellowship between you and your friends, not to mention the connection with HKUST School of Engineering! To extend our warm welcome and once again congratulate you on your graduation, we would like to offer you a new platform to stay in touch - SENG Alumni Club. Interested graduates may provide your personal information here. You have no obligation but to enjoy expanding your network for social and career growth in the years to come.

Although the phototaking service schedule might not suit yours, we still encourage you to stay connected through filling in the form. Should you have any queries, feel free to contact Vicky of the Dean’s Office (E:egvicky@ust.hk, T: 2358 8446).

See you at the Congregation and have fun on that day!

Warmest Regards,
Communications and External Affairs Team
